Introduction to Freestyle Rap

Freestyle rap is a spontaneous form of rapping where lyrics are improvised on the spot. It requires quick thinking, creativity, and the ability to flow with the rhythm. In this blog post, we'll provide an introduction to freestyle rap, its history, and the fundamental techniques to get you started on your journey to becoming a freestyle rapper.
